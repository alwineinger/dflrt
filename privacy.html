Privacy Policy — 3 Loop Relay

Effective date: July 28, 2025
Support

Overview

3 Loop Relay is an iOS utility for planning and tracking team relay races. You enter loop distances and runner paces; the app calculates projected start and finish times. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Summary: The app does not collect, track, store, or share personal data with the developer.

Data Collection

  • Data collected by the developer: None.
  • Storage: Race plans and any information you enter are stored locally on your device.
  • Transmission: The app does not transmit your data to developer-controlled servers. We do not operate any backend services.
  • Analytics/Ads: No analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies are used.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the iOS system share sheet to let you share your race plan (e.g., via Messages or Mail). When you choose to share, the selected app or service will process the shared content under its own privacy policy. 3 Loop Relay does not receive or retain that data.

Permissions

The app does not request access to your location, contacts, photos, camera, microphone, Health data, or other sensitive device information.

Children

3 Loop Relay is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Choices

Because we do not collect or control your data, there are no data access, correction, or deletion requests to process. You can delete the app to remove locally stored data.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, email us at dflrtguys@gmail.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the revised version here with an updated effective date.
